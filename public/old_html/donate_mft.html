Media Freedom Tokens (MFTs)

In addition to supporting our mission to fight for free speech and uncensored internet, your donation also entitles you to Media Freedom Tokens (MFTs), a bona fide cryptocurrency token that resides on the Ethereum blockchain.

  • MFTs are ERC20 compatible tokens that reside on the Ethereum blockchain.
  • Donors receive 1 MFT for every $1 US dollar they donate.
  • MFTs can be held as a long term investment, or they can be used to purchase goods and services from any organization that accepts them.

Chatflix Entertainment Group

Currently, the Chatflix Entertainment Group accepts payment in MFTs at par with the US Dollar (1 MFT = $1 USD). Chatflix offers unlimited streaming packages that let you watch over 50,000 premium movies and TV shows anywhere in the world, with no extra fees, no recurring billing, and no software to download.
